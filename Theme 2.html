Competency Project Hub

"Embracing Creativity and Practical Skills for Sustainable Development of Society"
RENEWABLE ENERGY

Bio-Waste Briquette Press

Reducing deforestation by turning charcoal dust and banana peelings into cooking fuel.

Practical Skill: Engineering a manual press.
Sustainability: Provides cheap energy while recycling organic waste.
WASTE MANAGEMENT

Plastic-to-Paver Conversion

Melting non-biodegradable plastics with sand to create durable garden tiles.

Practical Skill: Chemical ratio testing.
Sustainability: Cleans the local environment of plastic pollution.
CIRCULAR ECONOMY

Paper Charcoal Binder

Using school waste paper to create a durable binder for loose charcoal dust briquettes.

Practical Skill: Pulp-to-dust ratio experimentation.
Sustainability: Reduces school paper waste and provides fuel.
SANITATION

Solar Water Pasteurizer

Using a black-painted container and a simple indicator to kill pathogens using UV rays.

Practical Skill: Measuring heat absorption.
Sustainability: Safe water without burning wood or using chemicals.
WATER FILTRATION

Low-Cost Clay Filter

Combining local clay with sawdust to create porous pots that filter large water impurities.

Practical Skill: Pottery and kiln firing techniques.
Sustainability: Permanent, low-tech solution for rural clean water.
FOOD SECURITY

Hydroponic Fodder System

Growing animal feed in 7 days using only water and trays, requiring zero land acreage.

Practical Skill: Nutrient solution management.
Sustainability: Efficient land use for urban farming.
URBAN FARMING

Vertical Sack Garden

Maximizing vegetable production in small areas using sugar sacks and gravity irrigation.

Practical Skill: Drainage and stone-core engineering.
Sustainability: Lowers food costs and promotes urban resilience.
ORGANIC FARMING

Botanical Pest Spray

Developing a biodegradable pesticide using local garlic, chili peppers, and soap.

Practical Skill: Active compound extraction and testing.
Sustainability: Eliminates soil contamination from toxic chemicals.
SMART TECH

Soil Moisture Monitor

A programming project using Python or C++ to alert farmers when soil reaches a dry threshold.

Practical Skill: Coding logic and hardware interfacing.
Sustainability: Conserves water by ensuring precise irrigation.
DATA MANAGEMENT

Community Resource Hub

An offline-first web portal for villagers to locate local services like clinics or shops.

Practical Skill: UI/UX design and database management.
Sustainability: Connects community members to local sustainable resources.
